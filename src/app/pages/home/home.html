About Shepherds Inn

Rooted in Wells. Centered on Community.

Shepherds Inn has served Wells-area families for more than three decades. Our smaller setting allows residents to be known personally, form meaningful relationships, and remain connected to the community they call home.

Here, daily life feels familiar, personal, and connected.
