Services & Amenities

Supportive Assisted Living in a Warm, Home-Like Setting

At Shepherds Inn, residents receive personalized support in a warm, home-like assisted living community. Our goal is to help each resident feel safe, comfortable, engaged, and cared for while maintaining as much independence as possible.

Daily Living

Everyday comfort matters. Shepherds Inn helps residents enjoy familiar routines, nutritious meals, and a clean, welcoming environment.

🍽️ Home-cooked breakfast, lunch, and dinner served daily
🧺 Housekeeping and laundry support
🎲 Daily activities and social opportunities
🏡 Comfortable common areas for relaxing and visiting
🤝 Support with daily routines as needed

Personal Care & Health Support

Care at Shepherds Inn is personalized to each resident’s needs. Our team works to provide attentive support while encouraging independence, dignity, and comfort.

👩‍⚕️ Licensed RN nurse on staff
⏰ 24/7 on-site caregivers
📝 Customized care plans for each resident
💊 Pharmacy coordination and medication management support
❤️ Ongoing health monitoring
👨‍👩‍👧 Communication with families and healthcare providers as appropriate

Activities & Engagement

An important part of assisted living is staying connected. Shepherds Inn offers opportunities for residents to participate, socialize, and enjoy meaningful moments throughout the day.

🎨 Daily activities and group programs
☕ Opportunities to gather with fellow residents
🌼 Beautiful garden area for relaxing and visiting
🧩 Activities that encourage participation and engagement
😊 A welcoming environment where residents can join in at their own comfort level

Community & Belonging
