Services & Amenities

Personalized Assisted Living in a Close-Knit Wells Community

At Shepherds Inn, exceptional senior living starts with relationships. Our team takes time to know each resident personally – their routines, preferences, personalities, and needs – so support feels familiar, thoughtful, and responsive.

Families gain confidence knowing their loved one is cared for by familiar faces in a close-knit community where people genuinely enjoy what they do.

Comfort That Feels Like Home

Everyday life should feel familiar, not institutional. With meals, housekeeping, laundry, activities, and welcoming spaces all part of daily life, residents can spend less time worrying about chores and more time enjoying each day.

🍽️ Nutritious meals shared family-style around the table
🧺 Housekeeping and laundry services provided
🎲 Activities and social opportunities available daily
🏡 Comfortable common areas for relaxing and visiting
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family visits welcomed and encouraged

Personalized Care That Adapts With You

Every resident receives an individualized care plan built around their needs, preferences, and daily routines. As needs change, our team adjusts support thoughtfully – helping residents stay as independent, comfortable, and confident as possible.

📝 Individualized care plans for each resident
👩‍⚕️ Licensed RN nurse on staff
⏰ 24/7 on-site caregivers
💊 Pharmacy coordination and medication management support
👨‍👩‍👧 Communication with families and healthcare providers as appropriate
🛎️ Respite stays and short-term support available

🎨 Daily activities and group programs that encourage participation and engagement
🎉 Seasonal celebrations, holiday gatherings, and special community events
☕ Opportunities to gather with fellow residents
🌼 Beautiful garden area for relaxing and visiting
😊 A welcoming environment where residents can join in at their own comfort level
