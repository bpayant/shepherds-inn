About Shepherds Inn

A close-knit assisted living community rooted in Wells, Minnesota.

Shepherds Inn is a close-knit assisted living community rooted in the same neighborly spirit that has shaped Wells and the surrounding farming community for generations.

Here, residents can enjoy a smaller, more personal setting within the broader Wells community – a place where familiar faces, shared meals, daily routines, and local connections are part of everyday life.

Local roots. Personal setting.

In a town like Wells, it is not uncommon to meet someone whose family member, friend, neighbor, or loved one has been part of Shepherds Inn over the years.

Rooted in the Wells Community

Shepherds Inn traces its beginnings to 1987, when it started as a senior outreach program through Covenant Life Church. Since then, Shepherds Inn has continued to serve Wells and the surrounding area as a local option for seniors who want support close at hand without losing the familiarity of the community they know.

What makes Shepherds Inn feel different?

  • A smaller, more personal assisted living setting
  • Longstanding ties to Wells and the surrounding area
  • Shared meals, daily activities, and familiar routines
  • Comfortable spaces for visiting, relaxing, and gathering

Local Care, Experienced Leadership

Shepherds Inn is managed by Next Level Senior Living, a senior living operator built around a simple belief: great care starts with genuine relationships.

Their team brings experience in assisted living, memory care, respite care, and senior living operations, while staying focused on the daily details that matter most to residents and families – familiar caregivers, responsive communication, steady leadership, and a community where people feel known.

Next Level’s focus

  • Residents who are known personally
  • Families who feel informed and heard
  • Caregivers who are supported in their work
  • Leadership that is present and accountable
  • Operations rooted in compassion, consistency, and respect
