More Than Three Decades of Caring for Wells Families

For generations, Shepherds Inn has been part of the fabric of Wells, Minnesota. What began in 1987 as a senior outreach ministry through Covenant Life Church has grown into a trusted assisted living community where local families continue to care for one another.

Today, while healthcare continues to change, one thing hasn’t: our belief that every resident deserves to be known personally, treated with dignity, and surrounded by people who genuinely care.

Supported by Next Level Senior Living

Shepherds Inn is supported by Next Level Senior Living, a Midwest-based senior living company dedicated to helping locally rooted communities thrive without losing the personal relationships that make them special.

Rather than operating from a distant corporate office, Next Level partners closely with community leadership by providing operational expertise, clinical support, leadership development, marketing, financial oversight, and proven systems that allow local teams to focus on what matters most—caring for residents and supporting families.

At Shepherds Inn, that means combining the trusted reputation and community roots built over decades with the resources, experience, and continuous-improvement philosophy of Next Level Senior Living.

The Next Level Difference

At Next Level Senior Living, we believe exceptional senior living is built on four simple principles:

❤️ Residents Come First

Every decision begins with what’s best for the people who call our communities home.

🤝 Relationships Matter

We believe residents, families, and employees deserve to be known personally, not treated like numbers.

🏠 Local Leadership Wins

Great communities are built by empowered leaders who know their residents, their employees, and their hometown.

📈 Strong Systems Support Great Care

Behind every exceptional community are reliable processes, accountability, and continuous improvement that allow caregivers to spend more time caring for people.

The Next Level goal is simple:

Preserve everything that makes Shepherds Inn special while continually investing in the people, leadership, and systems that help our residents, families, and employees thrive.
